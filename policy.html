Refund and Cancellation Policy for RoxStar Karaoke Application

Last Updated: 28-08-2024

1. Refunds

1.1 Eligibility for Refunds:

  • Refunds are available for purchases made directly through the RoxStar Karaoke application using Razorpay.
  • Refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of the purchase date.
  • Refunds are applicable only for unused credits, subscriptions, or in-app purchases. Refunds for partially used services will not be processed.

1.2 Non-Refundable Items:

  • Non-refundable items include completed song purchases, custom recordings, and other one-time services that have been fully delivered.

1.3 Processing Refunds:

  • Once your refund request is received and verified, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.
  • Approved refunds will be processed and credited back to your original payment method within 10 business days.

2. Cancellations

2.1 Subscription Cancellations:

  • Subscriptions can be canceled at any time. Canceling your subscription will stop any future payments, but it will not retroactively refund any payments already made.
  • You will continue to have access to premium features until the end of the current billing cycle.

2.2 Event Booking Cancellations:

  • Event bookings can be canceled up to 24 hours before the scheduled time. To cancel, please contact us at cloud@rockstarstudioz.com.
  • Cancellations made less than 24 hours before the event may not be eligible for a refund.

3. How to Request a Refund or Cancellation

  • To request a refund or cancellation, please contact our support team at cloud@rockstarstudioz.com with your order or booking number and the reason for your request.
  • We will respond to your request within 3 business days.

4. Changes to This Policy

4.1 Updates:

  • RoxStar reserves the right to update or change this Refund and Cancellation Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to our application or website.

4.2 Notification:

  • Significant changes to this policy will be communicated via email or through an in-app notification.
